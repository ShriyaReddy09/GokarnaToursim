Why to visit Gokarna ?

Gokarna places offer the best of both worlds as the combination of adventure and tranquillity makes for a riveting visit. Gokarna is home to serene beaches. You will love visiting the temples, beach hopping and water sports. Pilgrimage sites are the highlight of Gokarna.

 

1) Shree MahaGanapati Temple :

This is one of the holiest site for Shiva and finds its mention in several Puranas, Ramayana and the Mahabharata. The temple's first construction was by the king Mayurasharma of the Kadamba dynasty.

Ravana was in anger and threw a stone at the young boy. The stone attacked his head, and this was when Lord Ganesha appeared in his own identity, and Ravana realized how all the Gods had tricked him. So, the place where Shiva Lingam was placed was built and developed as the Mahabaleshwara temple.

2) Half Moon Beach:

Half Moon Beach is a small beach situated in Gokarna. It is separated from the Om Beach by a cliff from where tourists are bound to get an amazing view of the Arabian Sea.Numerous village huts in the classical Indian style can be spotted lined up by the beach giving it a rather traditional and homely touch altogether.

3) Naga Chruch :

Couples come to this temple in the hope of having children and ask Naga for help. When a cherished wish is fulfilled and a long-awaited child is born into the family, the happy parents thank the snake in every possible way and place a sign in one of the free niches indicating the date of birth of the child. Right next to the temple there is an old tree, under which there is a slab depicting two intertwined snakes, as a symbol of masculinity and femininity.

4) Om Beach:

The beach derives its name from its unique shape, which resembles the auspicious symbol 'Om'. Famous for its pristine beauty, golden sands, and clear blue waters, Om Beach is a perfect getaway for those seeking tranquillity and relaxation.

5) Kudle Beach :

Kudle Beach is one of the many beaches of Gokarna, situated only a short trek from Om Beach. A complete recluse, this beach offers the most silent and serene atmosphere to enjoy a sunset, a walk along the shore or simply sit and observe the never-ceasing tides.

6) Belekan Beach:

Belekan Beach is one of the most remote Gokarna Beaches. To reach here, you can take a boat ride from Gokarna Beach. Moreover, you can also spot dolphins from this beach if you get lucky. Located close to Moonlight Beach, Belekan Beach is one of the most tranquil spots in all of Gokarna.
